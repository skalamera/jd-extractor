👋 Getting Started

The best way to start is to click the ⚙️ settings icon inside your extension popup and customize your profile under the tabbed interface:

  • API Configuration 🧠 Provide your Gemini API key inside the Core Settings tab. This empowers Clyde to instantly answer complex application forms, audit your resume, and write tailored documents!
  • Clyde Desktop Integration 🖥️ Set your Clyde Host and Port. When configured, Clyde automatically syncs all your saved job descriptions (clips) and tailored documents back to your Career Cockpit local server on port 4593!
  • Upload Master Resume 📄 Upload your Master Resume (PDF) inside the Resume & Audits tab. From here, you can click Audit Master Resume to get an instant, AI-powered ATS compatibility evaluation (strengths, gaps, and actionable fixes).
  • Set Profile Details 👤 Enter your contact, address, and social links in the Profile Details tab, and set standard demographic / work visa responses under the Preferences tab. This makes auto-filling basic fields instantaneous.
  • Custom Q&A Pairs 💬 Add custom keyword-value matching answers inside the Preferences tab. If an application asks a unique question (e.g. "Github link" or specific clearance prompts), your custom answers will override AI-generation for absolute accuracy!

✨ Once saved, you are fully set up. Load any job site, and you will see your active tailored resume loaded in the toolbar popup!

✨ Core Features

🤖 AI Auto-Apply

Click AI Apply ✨ on any saved job. Clyde loads the application form and uses your resume + job context to autonomously autofill complex forms across Workday, LinkedIn, Lever, Greenhouse, Ashby, and more!

📂 Multi-Card Autofill

Clyde automatically detects complex lists (like Work Experience and Education rows) on Workday and SmartRecruiters. It handles pointer-events and expands the exact number of card rows needed to match your profile!

📝 Tailored Documents

Generate highly-optimized Resume PDFs and Cover Letter PDFs that match the target job description to bypass ATS keyword algorithms and highlight your most relevant projects.

📅 Precise Date Insertion

Clyde bypasses date format mask crashes (e.g. MM/DD/YYYY on Workday and certifications) by formatting dates properly and writing them natively in one shot instead of typing character-by-character.

🎙️ STAR Method Interview Prep

Nervous about behavioral rounds? Clyde analyzes your background and target role requirements to write highly structured behavioral stories matching the STAR method.

🗂️ Application Tracker

Keep your funnel organized. Seamlessly drag or move job cards between Pipeline, Saved, Tracker, and Archived. Filter by dates, search by keywords, and track your progress!

🧭 Navigation & Visibility Controls

The Clyde popup is organized into four main tabs to match your job search funnel:

  • 🗂️ Pipeline - Your inbox for new jobs. Review match scores, generate tailored documents, and hit "AI Apply". Set the active job (⭐️ icon) to provide context for AI tasks.
  • ❤️ Saved - Your favorites. Click the heart icon on any job to save it here for later.
  • 📈 Tracker - Jobs you've applied to. Update statuses (Applied ➡️ Interviewing) and group cards by date or status.
  • 🗄️ Archived - End of the line. Keeps your active dashboard clean.

🚫 On-Page Badge Visibility Controls

Hover over the Clyde on-page ghost badge and click the close ("X") button to trigger the instant visibility dropdown:

  • • Hide until next visit: Standard close. Hides Clyde on this page temporarily for this session.
  • • Disable on all pages: Persistently turns the Clyde badge off globally.
  • • Disable on this domain: Disables Clyde only on the current domain (e.g. workday.com), leaving it active on other portals.
Pro Tip: If you disable the badge globally or on a domain, you can easily turn it back on anytime! Go to your Settings ➡️ Core Settings tab, where you can uncheck global disabling or click Enable next to any blocked domain.
