👋 Getting Started

The best way to start is to open the extension Settings (the ⚙️ icon in the popup) and follow these steps:

  • Enter Gemini API Key 🧠 Provide your Gemini API key. This empowers jayobee to write cover letters, analyze roles, and auto-fill applications!
  • Upload Master Resume 📄 Upload the master resume that all tailored resumes will be based off of. You can upload it here on the settings page or at the top of the popup UI in the Active Resume section. While on the Settings page, you can use the ATS Readiness tool which will scan and audit your resume, giving it an ATS score (0-100%, with 100% indicating a perfect resume). The tool will provide feedback on strengths, weaknesses, and highly actionable steps to improve it.
  • Fill Out Profile Information 👤 Complete the Profile Information section. These details are used for the AI Apply feature to automatically fill out basic application fields.
  • Complete Additional Questions ❓ Fill out the Additional Questions section. This contains common questions asked on most job applications and is also essential for the AI Apply feature.
  • Add Custom Q&A 💬 In the Custom Q&A section, enter any specific question and answer pairings you want the AI Apply feature to be able to handle when autofilling applications.
  • Save All Settings 💾 Click the save button to lock in all your configurations.

✨ When you open the popup, you will now see an Active Resume loaded and you are ready to add your first job to the pipeline!

✨ Core Features

🤖 AI Auto-Apply

Click AI Apply on any saved job. jayobee will open the application page and use your resume + the job description to intelligently auto-fill forms on Workday, LinkedIn, Lever, Greenhouse, Ashby, and more!

📝 Tailored Documents

Generate perfectly tailored Resume PDFs and Cover Letter PDFs that match the specific job description, bypassing ATS filters and highlighting your most relevant skills.

📊 AI Match Scoring

Every saved job gets a match score (Excellent to Poor) based on your resume. Click the score to see a detailed Gap Analysis, including your top strengths, main gaps, and mitigation strategies.

🎙️ Interview Prep

Nervous about behavioral questions? Click Interview Prep to generate custom STAR method stories based on your experience and the specific requirements of the role.

🤝 LinkedIn Networking

Need a warm intro? Use the LinkedIn Message generator to instantly craft a personalized outreach message to recruiters or hiring managers.

🗂️ Application Tracker

Keep your search organized. Move jobs between Pipeline, Tracker (Applied, Interviewing), and Archived. Filter by dates, search by keywords, and track your progress!

🧭 Navigation & Workflow

The jayobee popup is organized into four main tabs to match your job search funnel:


  • 🗂️ Pipeline Your inbox for new jobs. Review match scores, generate tailored documents, and hit "AI Apply". Set the active job (⭐️ icon) to provide context for AI tasks.
  • ❤️ Saved Your favorites. Click the heart icon on any job to save it here for later.
  • 📈 Tracker Jobs you've applied to. Click the bookmark icon to start tracking, then use the dropdown to update status (Applied ➡️ Interviewing). Group by date or status.
  • 🗄️ Archived End of the line. Jobs marked as "Accepted" or "Rejected" move here to keep your active tracker clean.
Pro Tip: Click on the text preview of any job in the popup to expand it and read the full description! Click the title or company name to edit them directly.
