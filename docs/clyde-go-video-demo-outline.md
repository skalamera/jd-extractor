# Clyde Go: Complete Video Demo Outline

**Video Concept:** A fast-paced, high-value product walkthrough demonstrating a complete, frictionless job application workflow. The focus is on moving from "finding a job" to "submitting a highly tailored application" in under two minutes, showcasing Clyde Go's AI intelligence and seamless automation.

**Tone:** Confident, efficient, and professional.

---

## Scene 1: The Problem & Introduction (0:00 - 0:10)
**Visual:** 
Fast-cut montage: A messy Excel spreadsheet full of job links, a generic "Resume_Final_v12.pdf" file, and an endless, blank Workday application form. The screen blurs out, and the sleek golden yellow and charcoal black **Clyde Go** logo animates into the center of the screen.

**Voiceover:** 
"Applying to tech roles is broken. You’re bouncing between spreadsheets, generic resumes, and endless application portals. Meet Clyde Go: your AI-powered command center that manages your entire job search pipeline directly inside your browser."

## Scene 2: Setup & Resume Audit (0:10 - 0:25)
**Visual:** 
Screen shows the Clyde Go Settings page. The cursor clicks "Upload PDF" under the Master Resume section and selects a file. The cursor then clicks the "Audit Master Resume (AI)" button. A loading spinner briefly appears, followed by a detailed text box expanding to reveal a score (e.g., 85/100), top weaknesses (like "missing metrics"), and actionable improvement steps. 

**Voiceover:** 
"It starts with your Master Resume. Upload your PDF and run it through Clyde Go’s strict ATS AI Auditor to identify weak verbs and missing metrics before you ever send it to a recruiter. Set your demographic defaults and custom Q&A rules once, and you're ready to hunt."

## Scene 3: Sourcing & Intelligent Clipping (0:25 - 0:35)
**Visual:** 


**Voiceover:**Screen transitions to a job posting on LinkedIn (or a company careers page). The user clicks and drags to highlight the job description text. They right-click the highlighted text, and the context menu appears. They click **"Save to Clyde Go"**. A sleek dark toast notification slides in at the bottom right: *"Saved to Clyde Go ✓
"When you find a role you like, just highlight the description, right-click, and save. Behind the scenes, Clyde Go’s AI immediately goes to work."

## Scene 4: The Hub & AI Gap Analysis (0:35 - 0:50)
**Visual:** 
The user clicks the Clyde Go extension icon in the Chrome toolbar. The popup drops down, showing the "Clips" tab. 
The top card displays the newly clipped job. The title is a clickable link. Underneath, it shows the Company, Location, Archetype, and Salary.
The cursor hovers over a bright green badge reading **"★ 4.8"**. The user clicks it, and a "Gap Analysis" panel slides open, revealing the Top Strength, Main Gap, and a Mitigation strategy.

**Voiceover:** 
"Open the extension to see your newly clipped job. Clyde Go automatically extracts the title, company, salary bands, and role archetype. More importantly, it scores the job against your master resume using a strict 10-dimension matrix. Click your match score to instantly see your top strength, your biggest gap, and exactly how to mitigate that gap in an interview."

## Scene 5: Proactive Networking & Prep (0:50 - 1:05)
**Visual:** 
On the same job card, the user clicks the **"Network"** button. A green success toast appears. The screen cuts to a LinkedIn message box where the user hits 'Paste' (Ctrl+V)—a perfect, 300-character personalized outreach message appears.
The screen cuts back to the popup. The user clicks **"Prep"**. A new Chrome tab instantly opens, revealing a beautifully formatted golden-yellow HTML page titled "Interview Prep Guide," filled with 5 tailored STAR+R behavioral stories.

**Voiceover:** 
"Don't just apply—stand out. Click 'Network' to instantly generate a punchy, first-person outreach message to the hiring manager, copied straight to your clipboard. And when you get the interview? Click 'Prep' to generate a custom cheat-sheet of STAR stories mapping your exact experience directly to the job’s core requirements."

## Scene 6: The Generative Document Engine (1:05 - 1:20)
**Visual:** 
Back in the popup, the user clicks the **"Both"** button on the job card. The button shows a spinner. Suddenly, two new tabs open, triggering the Chrome "Save as PDF" print dialog. 
One tab shows a stunning, highly-styled CV. The second shows a matching Cover Letter. The cursor hovers over the default save name, showing it automatically named as *"Software Engineer - OpenAI - CV.pdf"*.

**Voiceover:** 
"Ready to apply? Click 'Both' to generate a hyper-tailored CV and Cover Letter. Clyde Go rewrites your summary and bullets to mirror the job description's exact vocabulary, strictly maintaining a professional first-person tone. It formats them beautifully and automatically names the files for you."

## Scene 7: AI Auto-Apply & DOM Injection (1:20 - 1:40)
**Visual:** 
The screen shows the actual job application form (e.g., Greenhouse or Lever). The user opens the Clyde Go popup and clicks the green **"AI Apply"** button on the active clip.
The popup closes. A sleek status overlay appears in the top right corner of the screen. We watch as the form magically fills itself out. Dropdowns select the right demographic data. Open-text questions are filled with well-written, first-person paragraphs.
The cursor highlights the "Resume Upload" field on the page—it shows a PDF is already attached. 

**Voiceover:** 
"Here is where the magic happens. Hit 'AI Apply' on the application page. Clyde Go takes over, traversing the form to inject your profile data. It reads custom application questions and answers them confidently in the first person. And because you just generated a tailored CV, Clyde Go automatically converts it into a raw, ATS-friendly PDF and attaches it to the form for you—zero downloads or dragging-and-dropping required."

## Scene 8: Pipeline Tracking (1:40 - 1:55)
**Visual:** 
The user reviews the filled form, scrolls to the bottom, and manually clicks "Submit Application."
They open the Clyde Go popup again. On the job card, they click the "Track" toggle switch. 
They click the **"Tracker"** tab at the top. The view shifts to show jobs grouped neatly by date. The user clicks the status dropdown on the card, changing it from "Applied" (Blue) to "Interviewing" (Yellow).

**Voiceover:** 
"Review the answers, hit submit, and flip the tracking switch. Clyde Go automatically moves the job into your integrated Tracker tab, grouping your applications by date. Update your pipeline status from 'Applied' to 'Interviewing' right from the dropdown, completely eliminating the need for external spreadsheets."

## Scene 9: Outro & Call to Action (1:55 - 2:00)
**Visual:** 
The Clyde Go golden bee logo appears on a dark charcoal background. 
Text appears: *"Stop applying. Start choosing."*
Subtext: *"Download Clyde Go today."*

**Voiceover:** 
"Stop tracking spreadsheets. Start choosing your next role. Install Clyde Go today."
